Our story

Caldwell’s magical little treasure room.

Walk in and the first thing you notice is how much there is to see. A suit of armor here, a wall of glowing glass there, crates of records and cases of estate jewelry, all kept by a friendly group of vendors who genuinely love this stuff.

Customers call it nostalgic, magical, and a little overwhelming in the best way. There are antiques from every era, smalls and bigs, and the kind of curiosities that make the whole shop glow a little brighter. The owners are known for being warm and accommodating, the sort who will happily stay a few minutes past close so you can finish your treasure hunt.

  • A wide range of quality antiques, vintage and collectibles, priced to take home.
  • Layaway on larger pieces, so nothing special has to get away.
  • Right downtown, steps from Indian Creek Plaza and easy to make a day of.

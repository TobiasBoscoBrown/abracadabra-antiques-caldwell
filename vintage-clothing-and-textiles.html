Vintage Clothing & Textiles

Vintage clothing, flannels, linens and textiles with real wear and real quality.

What it is

Racks and shelves of vintage clothing and textiles, from cozy flannels to linens and accessories, the kind of made-to-last pieces that are hard to find new.

Good for

Building a vintage wardrobe, costume and styling finds, or a warm flannel that already has stories in it.

What you will find

  • Vintage flannels and outerwear
  • Dresses and separates across eras
  • Linens, quilts and textiles
  • Hats, bags and accessories
